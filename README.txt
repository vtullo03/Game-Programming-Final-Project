--- CONTROLS ---
WASD for directional movement 
ENTER to interact/select

--- OBJECTIVE ---
The game is a JRPG/Monster collector. The goal is to fight through the little guys to get through
to the big guy, GOG LORD OF THE MOUNTAINS, that you see on the left when you spawn in the overworld.

If your monsters die then the game ends

--- CHEAT FOR DEBUGGING/GRADING ---
LEFT SHIFT + H will give the player's monster +2000 health
